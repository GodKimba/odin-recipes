Strawberry Banana Protein Shake

A healthy protein shake to satisfy not only your nutritional needs as well as your belly needs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla-flavored whey protein powder
  • 2 cups ice
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 large banana
  • 1 tablespoon natural peanut butter

Step

  1. Layer milk, protein powder, ice, strawberries, banana, and peanut butter in a blender in this order; blend until creamy and smooth.
